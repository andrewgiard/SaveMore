Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page outlines how SmartSaver handles your data and ensures security while using the app.

We do not store, collect, or share any personal information locally or on external servers. All data entered, including insurance details, is processed only within the app and is not retained after use. Users have full control over their data.
